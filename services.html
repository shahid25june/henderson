Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. A small renovation might take 4-6 weeks, while a custom home could take 6-12 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline for your specific project.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate with inspectors throughout the project. Our team is familiar with local building codes and regulations to ensure full compliance.

What's included in your estimates?

Our detailed estimates include all labor, materials, permits, and project management. We're transparent about costs and will discuss any potential variables that could affect the final price.

Can I make changes during construction?

While we prefer to finalize plans before construction begins, we understand changes sometimes occur. We'll discuss the impact on timeline and budget for any mid-project changes.

What warranties do you offer?

We offer a 2-year warranty on all workmanship. Many materials come with manufacturer warranties that we'll review with you. Specific warranty details are included in your contract.
