Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2008

Founded in 2008 by John Masters, BuildMaster Construction began as a small residential renovation company with just three employees. What started as a passion for quality craftsmanship quickly grew into one of the region's most respected construction firms.

Over the past 15 years, we've expanded our services to include commercial construction, large-scale residential projects, and comprehensive design-build services, all while maintaining our commitment to the values that built our reputation.
